Artifact/requested_count source rule
- requested_count comes from ARLS support-demand artifact scopes generated by base monthly upload.
- ARLS does not invent request_count from HQ workbook at this stage.

Success / partial-success / failure semantics
- success:
  - inspect/apply succeeded locally
  - Sentrix handoff succeeded for all processed scopes
- partial success:
  - some scopes succeeded
  - or some scopes were excluded as stale/unselected
- failure:
  - Sentrix handoff failed before any meaningful downstream success
  - or apply was blocked before handoff

Retry behavior
- ARLS persists preview batch lineage and normalized scope summaries.
- Sentrix handoff can be retried from the stored batch.
- Re-upload is not required just to retry transport/handoff.
